About us

I'm so excited to announce the opening of TRATTORIA, Cagayan newest restaurant! We are a family-owned and operated Italian restaurant serving up delicious dishes inspired by authentic, traditional recipes. We believe that food should be enjoyed with friends and family and that's why we strive to make our restaurant a warm and inviting place where people can come together and share good times. Our menu features a variety of Italian favorites, from lasagna and pasta to pizza and desserts. We also offer a selection of specialty dishes that incorporate fresh, local ingredients and flavors, so there's always something new to try. We also have a full bar with an extensive wine list, so you can enjoy a glass of wine with your meal. Come and join us for a unique dining experience that you won't find anywhere else. We look forward to seeing you soon!
